Mark Haller
Owner | Operator

Mark has been in the construction industry since 2000. His background consists of building homes in Alaska as an introduction to carpentry and general construction for 7 years. After leaving Alaska to pursue other ventures Mark operated a general contracting partnership in Austin TX. Over the course of 6 years in Austin Mark had the opportunity to work in renewable construction and solar energy systems. For 15 years Mark has acquired trade experience in the following fields: Framing, General Carpentry, Roofing, Siding, Windows, Gutters, Renewable Construction, Photovoltaic Energy Systems, Flooring, Electrical, And more!
